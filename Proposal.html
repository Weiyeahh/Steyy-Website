What is Steyy about?

Steyy is an AI-based chatbot that can be customized to resemble a person. The website will educate the users on how to use the platform, display blogs on some touching user cases, and support the platform itself.

Why is Steyy important?

We all have someone that we wish could have stayed. Steyy is another way to bring them back.

Who's the audience?

Anyone whose' loved ones could no longer talk to them, such as who recently had a diseased relative, who just underwent a breakup, who lost a pet and many more cases!
