Steyy

"Is there anyone you wish that could have stayed?"

Steyy is an advanced AI-powered algorithmn that creates an virtual replica of the uploaded entity, be it a pet, a friend, a significant other, or a diseased loved one.

Steyy takes multiple dimensions to replicate the person:

Personality | Relationship | Gender | Age
As well as intakes specific materials such chat history, social media data, pictures and videos to create more a accurate replication. All materials are optional, the more materials provided, the more lively and accurate the replica would be.
